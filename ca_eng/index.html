Ciencia Aplicada

Is a postgraduate institution and consulting firm dedicated to providing forensic services, including training, trial consultation, expert testimony, as well as educational programs.

It is a consortium of internationally renowned forensic experts working in the areas of criminal and forensic psychology; criminal profiling; forensic criminology; and forensic science.

TRAINING + FORENSIC CONSULTING + POSTGRADUATE INSTITUTE

They have given training, and worked both criminal and civil cases, for governments, universities, and attorneys on 4 continents, to include the following nations: Canada, The United States, Mexico, Guatemala, Panama, Colombia, Ecuador, Chile, Argentina, Brazil, Scotland, England, Portugal, Spain, Australia, Singapore, South Korea, and China.
